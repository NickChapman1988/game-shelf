About

Game Shelf aims to make board games simple. There are literally thousands of games out there, so it's sometimes hard to know where to start when it comes to finding your next (or maybe first!) game. To help with that, Game Shelf tries to cut out the clutter and instead provide key information and, perhaps more importantly, honest reviews from other players to help you decide what you want to play.

You can browse and search our Games and read the reviews other players have written, as well as how each game is rated by the community. If you want to add your own ratings and reviews for games you've played, you can register an account and start straight away! It's free, and allows you to add ratings and reviews for any of the games in our catalogue.

Once you've reviewed a game, your rating will show up on the Games page so you can see how your rating compares to the community average rating. Your review will also be added to the relevant game page for others to read too. You can add more reviews, and edit or delete existing reviews, from your Profile page.

We hope you find Game Shelf useful. Now go play games!
