Welcome

Welcome to Game Shelf!

Game Shelf is a board game review site - a place for players new and old to find games they are interested in playing, read what others have said about those games, and rate your favourites!

Our Games page shows every single game ranked according to user ratings, so you can quickly find "the best" games!

If you've played something lately, why not leave a review for it? Registered users can add reviews for games and rate their favourites too!

And if you're in the market for a new game and find one you like the look of, all games have a 'Buy' link to where you can purchase from a friendly local games store!
